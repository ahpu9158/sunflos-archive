## Spell Baseline and Convention
With all historical background, there exists a couple of spell descriptions system. Most of these explanation will use Garoll's convention, which is widely use for Generalization Approach of magic.

### Garoll's convention
This convention treats magic like music. There is a lot rules and convention, but it leaves room for creativity.
